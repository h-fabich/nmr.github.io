83rd NMR2 Reunion Meeting March 23, 2024

About NMR²

Since 1982, the NMR spectroscopists in the New Mexico area have been meeting about twice a year. The meetings are always on Saturdays, include talks, posters, lunch, and an after-meeting activity. A complete list of dates and speakers is available here. Pictures from previous meetings are here.

87th New Mexico Regional NMR2 Meeting

Date: Saturday, June 6, 2026

Location: Room 101 (Auditorium at the north end of the building) of Clark Hall at UNM in Albuquerque

Speakers:

  • Michael Malone (LANL)
  • Steven Beyea (Dalhousie University)
  • Shin Utsuzawa (Schlumberger)
  • Rob Tycko (NIH/NIDDK)

Schedule:

  • 8:30 am Bagels, Juice and Coffee -- Poster set up.
  • 9:00 am Shin Utsuzawa: NMR well logging: Principles and Challenges
  • 10:00 am coffee
  • 10:30 Steven Beyea: TBD
  • 11:30 am Introduction of Vendors – Posters.
  • 11:45 am Lunch will be at Slate Street Cafe - hosted by Bruker.
  • 2:00 pm Michael Malone: Nuclear Quadrupole Resonance: From Explosives to Fentanyl
  • 3:00 pm Break -- cookies, posters, general discussion.
  • 3:30 pm Rob Tycko: Probing the Properties of Nanopore-Confined FG-Repeat Proteins from Nuclear Pore Complexes with NMR and AAO
  • Evening: Social Event at ABQMR. Hosted by Tecmag.



Please join us if you have a connection to - or an affinity for - New Mexico NMR!
There is no registration fee, but please notify the organizers of your desire to attend by emailing h.fabich@abqmr.com.
We hope to see you there!
