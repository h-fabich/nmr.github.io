Andrew McDowell

Andrew McDowell PhD, Physics, Cornell University (1993) is on sabbatical leave from Knox College (www.knox.edu) where he is an Associate Professor in the Department of Physics.  Recently at Knox he has focused on studies of hydrogen bearing quasicrystals using NMR.  His expertise is in the use of relaxation rate and spectroscopic measurements to study material properties such as structure, electronic structure, as well as the transport of hydrogen in materials.  Part of his effort is aimed as improving the theories linking the measured NMR parameters to the physical properties of interest.   He also has experience in the design and construction of experiments, including hardware and software for NMR and MRI spectrometers.    He has reviewed articles for Physical Review B, Journal of Magnetic Resonance, and Journal of Alloys and Compounds.
