New Mexico Resonance consists of a regular staff of scientists and an administrative officer plus visiting scientists, postdoctoral fellows, research assistants, students, and collaborators from universities and national laboratories. We develop techniques for studying flows by NMR imaging and apply them to flows in biology, physics, and engineering.
