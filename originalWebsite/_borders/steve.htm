Stephen A. Altobelli

Ph.D, Aeronautical/Astronautical Engineering, Ohio State (1982), is a Scientist with expertise in NMR imaging, hydrodynamics, blood flow, and computing. He is a reviewer for J. Appl. Physiol., J. Rheology, and ASME Trans. Biomedical Engineering, as well as DOE/SBIR proposals, and is Adjunct Professor of Mechanical Engineering, University of New Mexico.
