NMR images of the tagging experiments for the 1:1 water (bottom) and oil (top) mixture in the cylinder, which rotates clockwise. The rotation rate is 0.5 rev/s, and the tagging delay times Td  are (a) 1 ms, (b) 25 ms, (c) 50 ms, and (d) 100 ms. The interface between fluids is clearly shown. So is the misalignment in the horizontal direction caused by the chemical shift between water and oil. There is surprisingly little movement of either liquid, except for a thin layer near the cylinder that rotates with the cylinder and a layer along the water-oil interface in the center that flow to the right.
